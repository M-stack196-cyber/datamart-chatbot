Why We Exist

Most engineering teams are stretched thin — too much roadmap, not enough hands. Datamart was founded to close that gap without the overhead of a traditional agency or the risk of an unvetted freelancer.

We place senior, pre-vetted engineers directly into your workflow in 1-2 weeks, and back them with a US-managed delivery process so nothing falls through the cracks. Whether it's a single contractor or a full product team, the bar for quality stays the same.

Today that means staff augmentation, MVP builds, SaaS maintenance, and increasingly, agentic AI and automation — RAG pipelines, voice agents, and workflow systems that do real work, not just demo well.
